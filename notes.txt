# Step 1: Open the Website

Open AI FunVerse using the following link:

https://ai-funverse.vercel.app/

You will be redirected to the homepage of the platform.

---

# Step 2: Explore the Homepage

The homepage contains:

* Navigation Bar
* Hero Section
* Featured Experience
* Experience Cards
* Instructions
* Footer

Read the information to understand the different AI-powered experiences available.

---

# Step 3: Choose an Experience

Scroll to the **Explore Experiences** section.

You will find the following experiences:

* Harry Potter Invisible Cloak
* Neon Air Drawing
* Color Ball Hunter
* Rock Paper Scissors
* Magic Zone
* Live Puzzle

Click the **Play** button on any experience you want to explore.

---

# Step 4: Allow Camera Permission

When prompted by your browser:

Click **Allow Camera Access**.

The selected experience will start after the camera is enabled.

For the best performance:

* Use a well-lit room.
* Keep your hands clearly visible.
* Stand about 1–2 meters from the camera.

---

# Step 5: Harry Potter Invisible Cloak

Open the **Harry Potter** experience.

Steps:

* Click **Capture Background**.
* Step out of the camera frame while the background is captured.
* Return in front of the camera.
* Hold a red cloth or towel in front of your body.
* Watch the invisible cloak effect appear in real time.

---

# Step 6: Neon Air Drawing

Open **Neon Air Drawing**.

Using your hand:

* Raise your index finger to start drawing.
* Move your finger in the air to create glowing sketches.
* Use the available controls to change brush color and size.
* Clear the canvas whenever required.

---

# Step 7: Color Ball Hunter

Open **Color Ball Hunter**.

Gameplay:

* Floating color balls will appear on the screen.
* Use only your **index finger** to touch the balls.
* Each successful hit increases your score.
* Avoid missing too many balls.
* Try to achieve the highest score before the timer ends.

---

# Step 8: Rock Paper Scissors

Open **Rock Paper Scissors**.

Gameplay:

* Show Rock, Paper, or Scissors using your hand.
* The AI selects its move automatically.
* Each match consists of **5 rounds**.
* The player who wins the most rounds is declared the winner.
* Click **Play Again** to start a new match.

---

# Step 9: Magic Zone

Open **Magic Zone**.

Steps:

* Allow camera access.
* Raise your hands in front of the camera.
* Move your hands to activate magical visual effects.
* Explore different hand movements and enjoy the interactive animations.

---

# Step 10: Live Puzzle

Open **Live Puzzle**.

Steps:

* Create a square using the index finger and thumb of both hands.
* The selected area is captured automatically.
* The captured image is converted into a shuffled puzzle.
* Use your **index finger** to drag and place each puzzle piece.
* Complete the puzzle to display the **Winner** message.

---

# How It Works

### Step 1

Open AI FunVerse and choose any experience.

### Step 2

Allow camera permission.

### Step 3

The browser detects your hand movements and camera input in real time.

### Step 4

The selected AI experience starts instantly without any software installation.

# Tips for Best Results

* Use the platform in a well-lit environment.
* Keep your webcam clean.
* Allow camera permissions when requested.
* Keep your hands clearly visible inside the camera frame.
* Use Google Chrome for the smoothest experience.
* Close other applications that may be using your webcam.
* Refresh the page if the camera does not start properly.
